If you answered yes to any of the above, read more about how you can contribute below.

About the study
You are invited to participate in a research study that is testing a new medication for the treatment of chronic insomnia. The study aims to develop an understanding of how this medication affects insomnia as well as a range of health outcomes including anxiety, pain and quality of life.

Eligibility Requirements
You may be eligible to participate in this study if you:
• Healthy male and females aged 40-65 years old
• Proficient in English and have internet access and an active email account
• Mild to moderate insomnia experienced consistently for a period of 3 months
• Based in Sydney, NSW
• Willing to comply with all study procedures and are available for the 12-week duration of the study.


What’s involved
This study will take place over a 12-week period. You will be required to complete the following:
• Take a daily supplement of the study drug or placebo over the 12 week period,
• 1 x Phone call with the clinical trial coordinator (approx. 30 minutes),
• 2 x Pathology tests (blood test, urinary drug screen and a pregnancy test (if applicable)) at the start and close of the study (approx. 15 minutes each),
• 1 x Tele-health consultation with the study doctor (approx. 20 minutes),
• 2 x 30-minute online questionnaires, one at the start and one at the close of the study,
• 5 x 20 minute online fortnightly questionnaires
• Complete a 5-minute online sleep dairy and wear a sleep tracking watch during Weeks 4, 8 and 12.


You will be reimbursed for your time commitment to the study.

Eligible participants will be contacted and invited to continue the screening process.
